Leaning tower of Pisa

Is the leaning tower of Pisa really worth the visit? I believe so! The leaning tower of pisa seemed to have been such a big deal to me as a child and growing up, I realized that it's not something everyone gets to see in person in thier life time. It is located in Tuscany and is quite a breath taking sight to see. Plus, it will be a cool photo to post or show to friends and family! Sure, that may not be the most common reason to go, but it is a reason if any! This tower is pretty impressive with how long it has stayed standing.
